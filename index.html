Mental State Visualization

Answer the following questions:

  1. How have you been feeling lately?
  2. Rate your stress level today (1-10):
  3. How well did you sleep last night?
  4. Are you facing any challenges at work/school?
  5. How supported do you feel by family and friends?
  6. Have you had any joyful moments lately?
  7. What are you grateful for today?
  8. How often do you take a break?
  9. Do you engage in activities that make you happy?
  10. How often do you feel overwhelmed?
  11. What hobbies do you enjoy?
  12. Do you talk to someone about your feelings?
  13. How is your physical health contributing to your mental state?
  14. Do you practice mindfulness or meditation?
  15. How do you cope with negative emotions?
